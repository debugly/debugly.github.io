Get 方式是HTTP最常用的请求方式之一，请求头如下：

```
GET /g?b=qq&k=2E0Js1NhkJLCpr40OibG6Jg&s=100 HTTP/1.1
Host: q.qlogo.cn
Accept: */*
User-Agent: QQ/32293 CFNetwork/893.13.1 Darwin/17.4.0 (x86_64)
Accept-Language: zh-cn
Accept-Encoding: gzip, deflate
Connection: keep-alive
```
